A subtle but decisive point: in the T0 theory \emph{two different} fractal dimensions appear that must not be confused.

Directly inserted into volume scalings, $\Df^{\,\text{space}} = 2.9999$ yields only a vanishingly small correction: $(2.9999/3)^{2.9999/2} \approx 0.99993$ --- practically one.
