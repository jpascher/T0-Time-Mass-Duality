\end{equation}

The weak coupling constant is:
\begin{equation}

\begin{equation}

\subsection{Gravity}

The gravitational coupling for two protons is:
\begin{equation}
\alpha_G = \frac{Gm_p^2}{\hbar c} \approx 10^{-38}
\end{equation}

Gravity is by far the weakest force.
