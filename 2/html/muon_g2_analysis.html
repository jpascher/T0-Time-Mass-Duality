Theory Overview

The T0-theory proposes a completely parameter-free prediction of anomalous magnetic moments for all charged leptons. Starting from a single geometric parameter ξ, the theory derives all physical quantities including the fine structure constant and lepton masses without empirical adjustment.

Important Note

This theory represents an alternative theoretical framework that differs significantly from the Standard Model. While the mathematical formalism is presented systematically, the physical interpretations and claims would require extensive experimental validation and peer review to be accepted by the scientific community.
