💻 Shor Algorithm: Mathematics of Factorization

🔐 The Factorization Problem

RSA encryption is based on a hard problem:

Easy: 3 × 5 = 15

Hard: 15 = ? × ? (when 15 is very large)

RSA-2048: Factorize a 617-digit number

🔍 Shor's Trick: Period Finding

📐 Mathematical trick:

Instead of factorizing directly, find the "period" of a function

Function: f(x) = aˣ mod N

Period r: f(x+r) = f(x) for all x

If r is known → factors of N can be calculated!

🔄 Example: N = 15, a = 7

f(0) = 7⁰ mod 15 = 1

f(1) = 7¹ mod 15 = 7

f(2) = 7² mod 15 = 4

f(3) = 7³ mod 15 = 13

f(4) = 7⁴ mod 15 = 1 ← Period r = 4!
