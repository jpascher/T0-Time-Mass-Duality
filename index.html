The T0-Time-Mass-Duality Model

A Theoretical Framework by Johann Pascher

The T0 model offers an alternative theoretical framework to understand fundamental physics, based on the concept of time-mass duality. In contrast to the conventional relativistic approach that assumes relative time and constant mass, the T0 model proposes absolute time and variable mass, introducing an intrinsic time field T(x,t).

This comprehensive collection of documents explores various aspects of the T0 model, from its mathematical foundations to applications in quantum mechanics, cosmology, and particle physics. The model offers explanations for phenomena such as dark matter, dark energy, and the anomalous magnetic moment of the muon without introducing additional particles or free parameters.
